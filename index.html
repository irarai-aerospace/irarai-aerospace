Ira RAI

I am a final year undergraduate at the Department of Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras. I am excited by the research in robotics, complex networks and artificial Intelligence specially geared towards spacecraft systems.

Currently, I am working with Prof. R.I. Sujith at IIT Madras, on developing methods for detecting higher order interaction in multivariate time series. I was involved in computational simulations of ordinal partition networks and recurrence plots to build a robust model.

During summer 2024, I worked as an intern at the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory. I worked on evaluating minimum months required for a satellite validation. In addition I prepared initial steps for studying the atmospheric profiles via Aqua MODIS data by evaluating statistical inferences and generating Monte Carlo Simulations.

In summer 2023, I worked in MeLoDe (Memory Logic and Devices) Lab in Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Bombay, with whom I worked to simulate DCGANs and build a hybrid DCGAN architecture which uses ferroelectric material for stochasticity.

Since 2022, I am also working with Prof Richard Gordan, where we worked on defining the limits for "Gravitational Lensing Stereoscopy".


NOTE: I am applying for graduate school for the Fall 2025 admit.
